My Projects

Mutts Munchies

I designed a dog brand named Mutts Munchies, which offers a range of high-quality, all-natural treats for dogs. The brand features a unique orange packaging that exudes a cheerful vibe with a white accent. The packaging showcases an image of a happy dog relishing a bowl of wet food, which adds to the brand's appeal. As a graphic designer, I am confident that Mutts Munchies will stand out on the shelves and provide pet owners with a trustworthy and delicious treat option for their furry companions.

Country Delights

I designed the label for Countryside Delights, a delicious jelly product. It's made with mixed berries and contains no added sugar, and comes in a transparent bottle with a white lid. The cream label features a picture of the mixed berries, and the jelly inside is a beautiful shade of red. Perfect for spreading on your favorite sandwich!

Lumiere Lux

I designed Lumiere Lux, a luxury candle label that exudes elegance and sophistication in every detail. The label features a sleek black and white design that creates a striking contrast, emphasizing the premium quality of the product.

To complement the label, I also designed business cards that incorporate the same black and white aesthetic.

Overall, I wanted Lumiere Lux to be a brand that represents timeless elegance and luxury. The black and white design creates a classic and sophisticated look that will appeal to discerning customers who appreciate the finer things in life.

Canadian Winter Coffee

I designed Canadian Winter Coffee with a focus on capturing the essence of the cold winter months in Canada. The packaging is simple yet elegant, with two options available: a soft blue pouch and a soft pink pouch. Each pouch features a beautiful image of the cold mountains in Canada, which adds a touch of natural beauty to the overall design. To convey the idea of morning time, I added a bird chirping on the cup of coffee icon on the pouch, which creates a sense of warmth and comfort. The result is a premium coffee brand that not only tastes great but also looks and feels great.

Moon Club

I created a wine brand called Moon Club for a company, St. Julian Winery. The concept behind this label was to convey that the wine is so exceptional that it will take you to the moon. To achieve this impression, I designed a grey label with a space man, and used bright neon pink for the word "Moon Club" and the lid. I wanted the label to stand out on the shelf and emphasize the brand name.

Mellow Drops Candy

As the designer behind Mellow Drops Candy, I aimed to create a candy brand that captured the soft, sweet and indulgent experience of eating marshmallows. I chose a color scheme of soft pink and purple for the packaging, to give it a feminine and playful feel. The marshmallow imagery was a no-brainer, as it perfectly represents the candy's flavor and texture. The logo design was crafted to be simple and memorable, using a playful font that captures the candy's lighthearted and fun nature. Overall, I am proud of the design I created for Mellow Drops Candy and I hope that people will enjoy the candy as much as I enjoyed designing it.

My Portfolio

About Me

Welcome to my graphic design portfolio! My name is Lizelda, and I'm a passionate graphic designer specializing in branding, packaging, and social media design. My particular interest lies in working with pet products, food, cosmetics, and luxury brands, but I am always eager to explore new industries and expand my skills as a graphic designer.

My work reflects my dedication to creating unique and effective designs that help my clients stand out in their respective markets. I believe that design should not only be aesthetically pleasing but also functional and purposeful.

Throughout my career, I've had the privilege of collaborating with a diverse range of clients, each with unique challenges and requirements. My approach to every project remains the same: to understand the client's vision, their target audience, and to craft designs that are both visually appealing and effective.

In my portfolio, you'll find a collection of my best work, including branding projects, packaging designs, and social media campaigns. I hope that it provides you with a glimpse into my style and capabilities as a graphic designer. Thank you for taking the time to explore my work, and I'm excited about the possibility of collaborating with you.
